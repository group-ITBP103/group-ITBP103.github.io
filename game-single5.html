Esport

Gameplay

Enjoy fantastic open-ended gameplay, touch down in the tropics and transport freight between twelve exotic islands in more than a hundred exciting missions. Only the most daring pilots will take the risk of accepting both highly lucrative and illegal missions from shifty clients.

Conclusion

It feels like an asset flip cash grab. This game has absolutely no soul in it. You fly from point A to point B, slowly making money so that you can upgrade / buy new planes that you can continue to fly from point A to B but to islands which are further away. That’s it. No story. No reason. Just grind to grind. Flying involves just pointing in the right direction and putting the controller down until you’re there. The only excitement this game offers is land, excitement being an exaggeration, it’s barely a challenge. The only music is one song in the main menu which means the whole time you’re pretty much listening to the engine sound loop. Graphics look uninspired like an asset flip, there isn’t even a pilot in the plane. When you’re walking around you can’t even walk around the islands or even the buildings on the runways. Wow. I’m glad I got this second hand. I feel very, very, very sorry for anyone who paid full price for this trash.
